Welcome to the computer science department

First of all its a great privilege to be able to welcome you to the computer science department and we hope you will enjoy your experience with Edge hill univeristy

We know the first week can be daunting especially with most of you leaving home for the first time and experienceing a whole new experience. This is why this app was created to try and ease your experience in the first weeks of univiersty life within the computer science department. This app will aim to give you information that will be helpful and help streamline your inintal experience of freshers week.

Brief intro into freshers

First of all what is freshers week/Induction?

Induction week

This is where you will get to grips with login information and have a brief overview of what your degree will be like. Dont be scared of this as it is just to get everyone up to speed as the real work will start week two. In the induction week it will be more based around team building exercises and getting to know your other class mates.

Freshers week

This is where you can join societies and there will be differnet events that will take place throughtout the week this can be anything if you think you will enjoy it you should join it. There will be some events that are recommend by the department that are linked below which could help you devlop more in your degree and also meet new people which also enjoy learning new things around the subject so click the button below to learn more about the events.
